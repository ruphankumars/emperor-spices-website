Green Cardamom Exporters in India

Emperor Spices is a green cardamom exporter based in Bodinayakanur, Theni district, Tamil Nadu — the trading heart of India's cardamom belt. We supply export-grade AGEB, AGB and AGS pods, decorticated seeds and ground powder to importers across the Middle East, Europe, North America and Asia, backed by FSSAI, ISO 22000:2018, HACCP, APEDA and Spices Board of India registrations.

Why Source Cardamom from Bodinayakanur

Bodinayakanur, in Tamil Nadu's Theni district, sits at the foot of the Western Ghats and functions as one of India's principal green cardamom trading centres. Freshly cured capsules from the surrounding Cardamom Hills estates arrive here within days of harvest, which means an exporter operating from Bodinayakanur can grade, sort and pack stock while aroma and essential oil content are still at their peak.

For an importer, that proximity translates into three practical advantages:

  • Fresher lots — shorter farm-to-pack intervals preserve the volatile oils that drive flavour.
  • Direct selection — we evaluate parcels at source rather than buying blind through distant intermediaries.
  • Honest grading — capsules are machine-sorted and hand-checked against declared size bands (8mm+, 7–8mm, 6–7mm) before sealing.

Emperor Spices combines this origin advantage with documented export processes, so the cardamom that leaves our facility matches the specification sheet your purchase order was written against.

Certifications and Export Compliance

International buyers need more than good cardamom — they need paperwork that clears customs and satisfies food-safety auditors. Emperor Spices maintains:

  • FSSAI licence for food business operation in India
  • ISO 22000:2018 certified food safety management system
  • HACCP hazard-control protocols across receiving, grading and packing
  • APEDA registration for agricultural export
  • Spices Board of India registration as a spice exporter
  • IEC Code for international trade

Every consignment ships with a Certificate of Analysis, and we prepare phytosanitary certificates, certificates of origin and the commercial documentation your destination market requires. If your buyers run their own inbound testing, we are glad to align our COA parameters with your acceptance criteria before the order is confirmed. For markets with specific labelling or import-permit requirements, share the regulation reference with our export desk and we will confirm the document set before quotation, so nothing surfaces at the port that was not settled at contract.

Shipping, Packaging and Payment Terms

We currently ship to buyers in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain, the UK, Germany, France, the Netherlands, the USA, Canada, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia and South Africa.

Packaging is matched to your supply chain: traditional jute bags in 25kg and 50kg, PP woven bags for container efficiency, vacuum pouches from 100g to 1kg for retail-ready or aroma-critical programmes, and export cartons for consolidated loads. Private-label printing is available on pouch formats.

Payment is accepted by Letter of Credit (LC), Cash Against Documents (CAD) or advance transfer, agreed per order. For a first transaction we typically recommend an LC alongside a pre-shipment sample approval, so both sides operate with full security. Send your target grade, volume and destination port, and we will respond with a complete offer including packaging and Incoterms options.
