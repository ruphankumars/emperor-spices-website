## The gap to close first: contact enrichment

Your sheet currently has, per company: **name + estimated annual value**, but the
**Country/City, Client_Name (decision-maker), Designation, and Contact columns are empty placeholders.**
You cannot run outreach to a company name alone — you need a *person* + a *channel*.

**Enrichment workflow (per priority company):**
1. Find the company on LinkedIn → identify the **Procurement Manager / Purchase Head / Import Manager / Owner**.
2. Get a business email (tools: Hunter.io, Apollo.io, RocketReach — free tiers cover small batches) or the
   company's general `info@`/`sales@`.
3. Fill the Client_Name / Designation / Contact columns in your sheet.
4. Prioritise by the **Value** column (highest estimated annual value first — e.g. the $10M+ UAE traders).

Do this for the **top ~20 companies by value per country** first, not the whole list.

## Channel 1 — LinkedIn Ads (ABM / company targeting)

LinkedIn is the ONE ad platform that targets by **company name + job title** — exactly your use case.

- **Campaign type:** Sponsored Content or Message Ads
- **Audience:**
  - *Company targeting:* upload a **Company List** (company names from your sheet — LinkedIn matches them to its company pages). This stays within LinkedIn's ad tool; it is company *names*, not the personal/contact data you're protecting.
  - *Job titles:* Procurement Manager, Purchasing Manager, Import Manager, Category Buyer, Owner, Founder, Director (Food/Spices/Trading)
  - *Geo:* UAE, Saudi, Kuwait, Qatar, Oman, Bahrain, Malaysia, Singapore, Bangladesh, Germany, UK, Netherlands
- **Budget:** LinkedIn min ~$10/day; CPCs are high ($5-9). Use it as a *precision* layer on your highest-value targets, not mass reach. Even $300-500 over a month, tightly targeted, reaches the exact buyers.
- **Creative angle:** "Direct green cardamom from Bodinayakanur — AGEB/AGB/AGS, 500kg MOQ, samples with COA. Skip the middleman." CTA → WhatsApp or a short quote form.

> Decide separately whether you fund LinkedIn — it's NOT covered by the Google Ads credit.

## Channel 2 — Direct outreach (email + WhatsApp) — highest ROI, free

Once enriched, send a short, specific B2B intro. Templates:

**Email (subject: Direct green cardamom supply — Bodinayakanur, India):**
> Dear [Name],
> I'm reaching out from Emperor Spices, a green cardamom exporter based in Bodinayakanur, India — the
> heart of the cardamom belt. We supply AGEB (8mm+), AGB and AGS export grades direct from origin, with
> FSSAI, ISO 22000:2018 and HACCP certification.
> As [Company] imports cardamom into [Country], I'd like to send you our current grade specifications and
> a sample with Certificate of Analysis. Our MOQ is 500kg, with LC/CAD terms.
> Could I share a quote for your next requirement?
> [Name] · Emperor Spices · WhatsApp +91 97900 05649 · emperorspices.com

**WhatsApp (shorter):**
> Hello [Name], Emperor Spices here — green cardamom exporter from Bodinayakanur, India. AGEB/AGB/AGS
> grades, 500kg MOQ, samples with COA. May I send you our specs & today's offer for [Company]?

**Cadence:** Day 1 email → Day 4 WhatsApp/LinkedIn connect → Day 9 follow-up with a specific price
indication → Day 16 final nudge with a sample offer. Track status in your sheet's Status column
(In progress / Contacted / Completed — already set up).
