Premium Indian green cardamom from Bodinayakanur, Tamil Nadu. All grades AGMARK certified.

## Whole Green Cardamom Grades

### AGEB — Extra Bold
- Pod size: 8mm and above
- Essential oil content: 7-8%
- Moisture: below 10%
- Color: deep green
- Best for: premium retail, luxury gifting

### AGB — Bold
- Pod size: 7-8mm
- Essential oil content: 6-7%
- Moisture: below 10%
- Color: bright green
- Best for: Middle East and European markets

### AGS — Small
- Pod size: 6-7mm
- Essential oil content: 5-6%
- Moisture: below 11%
- Color: green
- Best for: food processing, hospitality (HORECA)

### Cardamom Seeds (Decorticated)
- Essential oil content: 8-10%
- Moisture: below 8%
- Color: brown-black
- Best for: essential oil extraction, pharmaceutical use

### Cardamom Powder
- Essential oil content: 6-7%
- Moisture: below 10%
- Color: olive
- Best for: spice blends, bakery
