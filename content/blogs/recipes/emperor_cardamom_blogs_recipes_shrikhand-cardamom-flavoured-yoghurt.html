Shrikhand / Cardamom Flavoured Yoghurt

Shrikhand is a sweetened yoghurt infused with cardamom and enriched with dry fruits for a rich, wholesome flavor.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Plain yogurt (thick)
  • 1 tsp Green cardamom powder
  • 1 tsp Green cardamom (crushed)
  • 3/4 cup Refined sugar
  • 1/4 cup Assorted dry fruits (almonds, cashew nuts, and black raisins)
  • Large muslin cloth or cheesecloth
  • A few saffron strands for garnishing (optional)

Instructions

  1. Drain the yogurt using a muslin cloth for 3 hours to remove all water.
  2. Transfer the thickened curd to a bowl and add cardamom powder and crushed cardamom.
  3. Beat the mixture for about 3 minutes until smooth and creamy.
  4. Add sugar and blend for another 5 minutes until fully dissolved.
  5. Mix in dry fruits and garnish with saffron.
  6. Refrigerate and serve chilled.
