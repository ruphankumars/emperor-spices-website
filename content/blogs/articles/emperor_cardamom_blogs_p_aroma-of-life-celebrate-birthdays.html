Emperor Spices Cardamom: The Essence of Goodness for Birthday Delights

Cardamom (Elaichi) is a spice that adds a touch of magic to culinary adventures, especially when it comes to birthdays. Emperor Spices Cardamom brings along the best quality cardamom, with big pods, more seeds, and an intoxicating aroma.

Swedish Cardamom Buns Recipe for Birthday Celebrations

Ingredients:

  • 30 whole Emperor Spices Cardamom pods
  • 350 ml milk
  • 200 g unsalted butter, cubed
  • 500 g strong white bread flour
  • 220 g golden caster sugar
  • 7 g dried fast-action yeast
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons sugar pearls

Step 1: Infuse the milk with cardamom - crack open 10 pods and add to saucepan with milk. Warm until steaming.

Step 2: Combine flour, sugar, yeast, cinnamon, and salt. Add cardamom-infused milk and mix until smooth dough forms.

Step 3: Crush remaining cardamom seeds and mix with butter for filling.

Step 4: Roll dough, spread filling, fold and cut into strips. Tie in knots and bake until golden.

Step 5: Brush with cardamom-infused syrup for extra sweetness.
