The Colour Grading Guide to Buying Cardamom

"One size fits all" goes the old cliché. However, in so many of life's situations, that is never an ideal. The same applies to cardamom - different applications require different grades.

Emperor Spices's Revolutionary Grading System

Emperor Spices Cardamom has introduced a unique five-color grading system based on pod diameter, making it easy for consumers to choose the right cardamom for their needs.

The Five Grades

Grade Pod Size Best Used For
Purple 8mm & above Premium gifting, luxury desserts
Pink 7.5mm & above Restaurant-quality dishes
Green 7mm-8mm Everyday home cooking
Orange 6.5mm-7.5mm Tea and beverages
Red 6mm-7mm Industrial and bulk use

Choosing the Right Grade

  • For gifting: Purple or Pink grade for maximum visual appeal
  • For biryani: Green or Pink grade for perfect flavor
  • For chai: Orange grade - cost-effective with excellent flavor
  • For desserts: Purple grade for show-stopping presentation

No matter which grade you choose, Emperor Spices ensures consistent quality and freshness.
