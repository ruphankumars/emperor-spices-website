Cardamom Exporters in Kerala: The Spice Route of the World

Kerala, often referred to as the "Spice Garden of India," has long been celebrated for its rich heritage in spice production. At the center of this legacy is cardamom, the Queen of Spices.

Kerala's Cardamom Legacy

For centuries, traders from around the world have sought Kerala's spices. The ancient spice routes that connected India to the Roman Empire, Arabia, and beyond were built on the allure of Kerala's aromatics.

The Alleppey Connection

Alleppey Green Cardamom, named after the historic port city of Alleppey (Alappuzha), is considered the world's finest cardamom. This variety is distinguished by its superior oil content, intense aroma, and distinctive green color.
