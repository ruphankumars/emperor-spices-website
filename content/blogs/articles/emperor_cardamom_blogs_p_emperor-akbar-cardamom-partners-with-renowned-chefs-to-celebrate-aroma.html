Emperor Spices Cardamom Partners with Renowned Chefs to Celebrate Aroma

Emperor Spices Cardamom is proud to announce an exciting collaboration with three renowned chefs to inspire home cooks with innovative cardamom-infused dishes.

Meet the Chefs

Chef Hala Ayash

A celebrated Middle Eastern cuisine expert, Chef Hala created a stunning Vegetable Kabsa showcasing how cardamom elevates this traditional Arabian rice dish.

Chef Mohamad Chabchoul

Known for his fusion approach, Chef Mohamad developed a Cardamom Pear Galette - a French-inspired dessert with an Indian twist that highlights the versatility of cardamom in Western baking.

Chef Ammar Albaraksati

A pastry specialist, Chef Ammar crafted Swedish Cardamom Buns (Kardemummabullar) - demonstrating how cardamom transcends cultural boundaries to appear in Scandinavian baking traditions.

The Universal Spice

This collaboration underscores what we've always known: cardamom is a universal spice that enhances cuisines across cultures - from Indian chai to Arab coffee, from Swedish pastries to French desserts.

Recipes Available

All three recipes are available on our website, complete with step-by-step instructions and tips from the chefs themselves.
