People tend to become slaves of their own habits - whether they're good or bad. What remains essential for a person's health and wellbeing is for them to self-analyse and reflect which habits work in their favour and which don't.

The Problem with Bad Habits

On a daily basis, one partakes in many habits that may bring temporary joy but eventually lead to permanent damages. Consuming cigarettes, tobacco, or pan masala are considered severe bad habits, but consumption of soft drinks and chewing gum after a certain level can also pose threats to a person's health.

Cardamom: A Healthy Alternative
