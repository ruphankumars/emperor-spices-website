2. How does the Cardamom Manufacturer handle grading and quality control?

Cardamom comes in different sizes, and grading by size is a common practice in the industry. However, it's crucial to understand that the grading is based on the diameter size of the pod, not the length.

Why it matters: Grading ensures that you get the right cardamom for your intended use, whether it's for making high-end teas, premium spice mixes, or large-scale production.
