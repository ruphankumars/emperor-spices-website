Emperor Spices Cardamom - A Royal Journey from Harvest to Export

Welcome to the world of cardamom, where Emperor Spices Cardamom reigns supreme as the top exporter in India and the largest exporter of cardamom worldwide.

From Field to Facility: Uncompromising Quality

Emperor Spices Cardamom takes pride in its state-of-the-art HACCP-certified processing facility. Immediately post harvesting, the cardamom pods undergo meticulous processing to preserve their freshness and aroma.

Graded for Perfection: Size Does Matter

Emperor Spices Cardamom has introduced a unique grading system based on the size of the pod diameter:

  • Purple Grade packs: 8mm & above pods
  • Pink Grade packs: 7.5mm & above pods
  • Green Grade packs: 7mm-8mm pods
  • Orange Grade packs: 6.5mm-7.5mm pods
  • Red Grade packs: 6mm-7mm pods

Innovating the Industry: Aroma-Lock Packaging

Emperor Spices Cardamom stands at the forefront of innovation with its pioneering patented Aroma-Lock packaging. Designed to seal in the plantation freshness, these specially crafted packets ensure that the aromatic essence and flavourful oils remain intact.

The Epitome of Excellence: Geographical Indication (GI) Tag

Emperor Spices Cardamom proudly holds the distinction of being the authorized user of the GI tag for Alleppey Green Cardamom.

A Triumph of Excellence: Awarded as India's Top Most Exporter

Emperor Spices Cardamom has been recognized as India's Top Most exporter of green cardamom for 2018-19 and 2019-20 by the Spices Board of India.

A World-Class Exporter: Spreading Aromatic Delights

Emperor Spices Cardamom has taken the world by storm, exporting superior-quality Alleppey Green Cardamoms to 25 countries and counting.
