Privacy Policy

Welcome to ESP ENTERPRISES LTD (“we,” “us,” “our”). We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.emperorspices.com (the “Site”) and interact with our service etc.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal and non-personal information:

a. Information You Provide:

  • Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Company name and job title (if applicable)
  • Messages or inquiries sent through contact forms

b. Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited, time spent, and referring URLs
  • Device type and operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see below)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or provide customer support
  • To improve and optimize our website and services
  • To send updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (only if you opt in)
  • To analyze website traffic and marketing performance
  • To serve personalized ads through platforms like Meta (Facebook/Instagram)

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve user experience
  • Track usage patterns
  • Provide targeted advertising through platforms like Meta

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. By continuing to use our site, you consent to the use of cookies.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics, advertising partners like Meta)
  • Law enforcement or legal authorities if required by applicable law
  • Internal stakeholders for business operations

5. Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Advertising

We may use Meta Pixel and other tracking tools provided by Meta to monitor the effectiveness of our ads and deliver better-targeted content. This may involve collecting limited information about your interactions with our website. Meta may use this data in accordance with its own privacy policies.

You can manage your preferences for ads shown to you on Meta platforms through your Facebook Ad Preferences.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party sites.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website. We encourage you to review it periodically.
